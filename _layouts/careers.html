Head of Marketing Solutions

Milan, Italy

Pitchtarget is a performance marketing platform. Our mission is to become the growth machine for business of any size. We want to make marketing easy to understand for marketers, and less painful for people. We use technology to make hard tasks easy, and we are enabling startups, ecommerces and media agencies to scale through our marketing platform. We have an all-round marketing intelligence that we apply by working side by side with our partners.

What you will be doing

We are looking for a talented and experienced Head of Marketing Solutions who grew reputation in the Performance Marketing field. Our Head of Marketing Solutions will help us bootstrap our Sales organisation, and to deliver on our budget goals. You have the chance to become our third founder, which means you will have no direct report, and you will have the opportunity to lead in creating a successful sales organisation that will break ground in both marketing and advertising. Your equity and compensation will closely depend on your impact. If you are gold, we'll give you gold.

Our Head of Marketing Solutions will be responsible for:

  • Managing relationships with existing clients
  • Offer training, onboarding and education through a constant relationship with our existing and new clients
  • Doing the impossible to acquire new clients
  • Qualifying existing leads
  • Study and implement scalable strategies to support our torso and tail clients
  • Working out forecasts and quotas in order to meet our budget goals
  • Influence the market and the industry by establishing relationships with stakeholders in the EMEA region

Expectations

Apply if:

  • You have a proven track record of exceeding your quotas
  • You built your reputation and your career on performance marketing
  • You are hungry. You crave performance and results.
  • You love to meet and help clients
  • You speak English

Bonus points

You will jump the queue if you have one or more of these:

  • You led a Sales or Marketing team
  • You know how to use Facebook Ads and/or AdWords, plus you know how RTB works
  • You sold digital advertising in your past life
  • You have an MBA or equivalent level of education
  • You can't take anymore of your job and you want to make tremendous impact. Don't watch the next big thing. Let's build it together.

About this position

  • This position will be based in Milan, Italy
  • This is a quota-based position with fixed salary and bonus on commission schedule
  • Your salary and bonuses will be determined based on experience and performances.
  • Benefits include (but are not limited to) laptop, phone, travel budget

Front-End Engineer

Venice, Italy

Pitchtarget is a performance marketing platform. Our mission is to become the growth machine for business of any size. What you will be doing

We are looking for a Front-End Engineer who is passionate about both product and technical challenges. A Front-End Engineer at Pitchtarget is responsible for:

  • Work together with the product team in order to prototype new ideas and features
  • Work with out Product Designers to transform mockup into awesome interfaces
  • Work together with our Engineering team in order to integrate your work into our development and release cycle
  • Re-thinking, refactoring, making things better
  • Challenging the rest of the team by being faster and smoother
  • Performing maintenance work and fixing bugs

Expectations

Apply if:

  • You built something that you are proud to show us
  • You have experience with a client-side MVC framework such as Angular, Ember, Backbone, ExtJS, plus you know jQuery
  • You know how to write CSS and Javascript according to coding standards
  • You know how to integrate REST APIs from different vendors in Javascript apss
  • You have a strong experience using Git in big teams
  • You do not compromise about quality. You are OCD about perfection. If something's not right, you just go ahead and change it.

Bonus points

You jump the queue if you have one or more of these:

  • You have experience with Javascript testing frameworks
  • You know Ruby on Rails
  • You know how to optimise performance and memory of complex Javascript apps
  • You have built or worked in a startup, or if you have proven records of successful team leading
  • You can prove examples of solving problems while looking at the big picture
