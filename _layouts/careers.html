Head of Sales

London, UK

Pitchtarget is a marketing PaaS. Our mission is to build better ways for businesses to connect with people. We want to make marketing easy to understand for marketers, and less painful for people. We use technology to make hard tasks easy, and we are enabling startups, ecommerces and media agencies to scale through our marketing platform. We have an all-round marketing intelligence that we apply by working side by side with our partners.

What you will be doing

We are looking for a talented and experienced Head of Sales that can help us bootstrap our Sales organisation. You have the chance to become our third founder and to break ground in both marketing and advertising. Your equity and compensation will closely depend on your impact. If you are gold, we'll give you gold.

Our Head of Sales will be responsible for:

  • Managing relationships with existing clients
  • Doing the impossible to acquire new customers
  • Bootstrapping the Sales organisation, including hiring, managing and training the sales team
  • Working out forecasts and quotas in order to establish sales goals
  • Optimising the sales process with your expertise

Expectations

Apply if:

  • You have a proven track record of exceeding your quotas
  • You have a background in digital marketing
  • You are hungry. You crave performance and results.
  • You love (and want) to travel about ~20% of the time
  • You speak English

Bonus points

You jump the queue if you have one or more of these:

  • You worked with media agencies and digital marketing
  • You sold Internet/enterprise software in your past life
  • You sold digital advertising in your past life
  • You have an MBA or equivalent level of education
  • You can't take anymore of your job and you want to make tremendous impact. Don't watch the next big thing. Let's build it together.

About Pitchtarget

  • We started in January 2014
  • We are part of H-FARM, they invested heavy on us. That's why we can afford a Head of Sales.
  • We are a team of entrepreneurs. Some of us left Facebook to build Pitchtarget.

Software Engineer

Venice, Italy

Pitchtarget is a marketing PaaS. Our mission is to build better ways for businesses to connect with people. We want to make marketing easy to understand for marketers, and less painful for people. Every day we face problems that are unique to this platform, and we are looking talented people who love to solve those challenges.

What you will be doing

We are looking for a Software Engineer who is passionate about both product and technical challenges. You should be confortable with the entire web stack, but if you lean towards backend technologies we would be thrilled to know you anyways. A Software Engineer at Pitchtarget is responsible for:

  • Designing and building software. This is not just opening and closing issues or tasks. You will be responsible for your code from inception to deprecation.
  • Brainstorming new features with the product guys.
  • Re-thinking, refactoring, making things better.
  • Challenging the rest of the team by being faster and smoother than them.
  • Performing maintenance work and fixing bugs.

Expectations

Apply if:

  • You built something that you are proud to show us.
  • You have a core understanding of fundamental computer science problems.
  • You mastered in one programming language.
  • You solve problems by looking at the big picture, rather than fixing the code only.

Bonus points

You jump the queue if you have one or more of these:

  • You worked with Google AdWords API, Facebook Ads API or Twitter Ads API.
  • You built ecommerces or travel/booking websites.
  • You know multiple programming languages (we write Ruby/Rails, Javascript, PHP).
  • You have built or worked in a startup, or if you have proven records of successful team leading.
  • You know what MongoDB is, and you are not afraid to use it with MySQL or PostgreSQL.
